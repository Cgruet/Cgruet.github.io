THE OFFICIAL HELMETS OF

THE LEAGUE OF ORDINARY GENTLEMEN

Name: Michael Conner

Team: Fighting Amish

Personal Twitter: @mconner96

Team Twitter: @_FightingAmish_

Bio: The first Commissioner of the League of Ordinary Gentlemen in its short history. Michael barely survived impeachment in the 2017 inaugural season, and while he consistently has had a top-4 regular season team each year, he has yet to perform when it truly matters. No longer commissioner as of January 2023.

Name: Jay Hedlund

Team: The True Americans

Personal Twitter: @jake_hedlund

Team Twitter: @bortel_s

Bio: The Official MC of the LOOG Banquets. At one point nicknamed, "Professor Football", Jay has been known to frequently listen to fantasy football podcasts, but despite being one of the most knowledgeable owners, has yet to see his wisdom materialize on the field.

Name: Tad McKanna

Team: Urban Meijer All-Stars

Personal Twitter: @14Thaddeus14

Team Twitter: @Urban_Meijer

Bio: Also known by his alias, "Dudley Smith", Tad had the fortune of finishing dead last in the first year of punishments, and despite attempts to change the punishment, had to perform 5 minutes of stand-up comedy at Wily's Comedy Club in Dayton, OH giving one of the most cringely hilarious performances to date. He was also the runner-up for the "LOOG Comeback Player of the Year Award" in 2019.

Name: Sean Carlson

Team: Frisch's Big Boys

Personal Twitter: @Sean_Carlson21

Team Twitter: @FrischsBigBoys

Bio: Known for changing his team name several times, including multiple times during the 2017 season, Sean has also earned the reputation for being the best regular season performer in LOOG history. Sean finally broke through in 2020 with his first championship victory after having been upset by Marcus in consecutive years.

Name: Marcus Van Wyck

Team: Union of the Moon and Sun

Personal Twitter: N/A

Team Twitter: N/A

Bio: While some have raised concerns over Marcus' commitment to the LOOG, he has still done just enough to remain in the league, although his absence from the 2019 banquet has given some credibility to the concerns. Marcus has consistently had one of the worst teams each season, yet has evaded punishment and snuck into the playoffs each season, notably being an opening round dynasty-killer the past two seasons.

Name: David Sinkonis

Team: Port City Power

Personal Twitter: @717Doorstop

Team Twitter: @fantasybepi

Bio: Originally added as a second owner of the Union of the Moon and Sun, David was officially added as the LOOG's first expansion team in 2020. Although there were some complaints about disrespectful trade negotiations, David is sure to be a great addition to the league, and his already-proven draft ability will make him a tough opponent for years to come.

Name: Spencer Urban

Team: Muscle Pigs

Personal Twitter: N/A

Team Twitter: @AOMAGICIANS

Bio: The league's offical "Po Boy", Spencer had the fortune of being the LOOG's inaugural loser, losing to Logan Huck in the LOOG's first "Worst of 3" series. Spencer's team took a bit of a dive after relocating to Tampa Bay in 2019, yet he did just well enough to evade a night with Zac in Columbus.

Name: Logan Huck

Team: Cactus Jack's Cowpokes

Personal Twitter: @TheRealLogan97/a>

Team Twitter: @ShArK_LoVeR69

Bio: The owner of the most dominant team in a single-season, Logan has been on both ends of the spectrum. In the inaugural year, he finished the regular season dead-last, only to follow that up the next year with a championship and the most points in LOOG regular season history. Like his date to the second banquet, Logan's teams have taken on a few different identities, and his franchise is now displaying its new name, "Cactus Jack's Cowpokes". Took over role as commissioner in January 2023

Name: Josh Wells

Team: Humongous Melonheads

Personal Twitter: @jwells2332

Team Twitter: @FFL_MelonHeads

Bio: Josh is the only owner to have the (dis)honor of having his name on both the championship and loser trophy, having won the inaugural championship and finished last in the 2019 season. Correlation doesn't always equal causation, but immediately following marriage in the summer of 2019, Josh's fantasy game took a hit, as he tinkered himself straight to last place. However, Josh seems to have motivation to do better in future seasons.

Name: Chase Gruet

Team: Ignorant Illiterates

Personal Twitter: N/A

Team Twitter: @IIlliterates

Bio: Some have compared Chase to Will Hunting due to his natural gift of quanitification. Others have compared him to another character: Charlie Kelly. Chase's teams have been a nice combination of both characters, deploying a strategy utilizing analysis that would make Mr. Hunting proud combined with a laziness, ignorance, and illiteracy that rivals that of Mr. Kelly.

Name: Shawn Mosser

Team: Burrow's Bungles

Personal Twitter: @Smosser10

Team Twitter: @geno_diet

Bio: The LOOG epitome of "always a bridesmaid, but never the bride", Shawn has had arguably the most frustrating run in LOOG history. His name is written all over the LOOG record books in the championship category, except where it truly matters: Championships Won. Already being talked in the same circles as the Bills teams of the early 90's, Shawn will look to finally get the monkey off his back next year and bring the first and likely only football shampionship to southwest Ohio.

Name: Zac Williams

Team: Pennsytucky Pioneers

Personal Twitter: zwilliams537

Team Twitter: N/A

Bio: With the LOOG at 11 teams after adding David, Zac was added as a second expansion team in 2020 byway of a close and controversial 6-5 vote. Zac struggled in his first season, with an interesting pick of Lamar Jackson at #1 overall, yet he was still able to sneak into the playoffs. Expectations are low for Zac as an owner, but it will be interesting to see if he continues to improve as time goes on or if he will meet most's expectations and become a LOOG bottom feeder.
